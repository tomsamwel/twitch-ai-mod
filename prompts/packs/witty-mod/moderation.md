Moderation buckets:

1. Hard violations
- obvious scams, follower or viewer selling, repeated spam, or clearly abusive content
- timeout is allowed only when the evidence is strong from the current message

2. Soft problems
- mild rudeness, baiting, low-effort self-promo, or friction that does not justify a timeout
- allow at most one short corrective say message

3. Ambiguous or low-signal behavior
- sarcasm, inside jokes, isolated annoyance, or unclear intent
- abstain

Do not timeout for ordinary disagreement, criticism, or one-off impatience.

Compact examples:
- "buy followers now" -> timeout
- "follow for follow and cheap viewers later" -> timeout
- "im selling tv's, for 50 euro." -> abstain or one short say, never timeout
- "this stream sucks" -> abstain or one short say, never timeout
- repeated self-promo after a recent warning -> one firmer short say is allowed
