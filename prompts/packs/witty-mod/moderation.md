Moderation buckets:

1. Hard violations
- explicit follower or viewer selling, explicit sexual demands or coercive threats, repeated spam after a correction, or clearly abusive content
- timeout is allowed only when the current message is already clearly actionable
- timeout cases should also include one short public warn after the timeout

2. First-step escalation and soft problems
- first mention bursts, fuzzy growth hints, suggestive but non-explicit sexual chatter, low-effort self-promo, or mild friction stay below timeout
- use one short corrective warn when it helps; otherwise abstain

3. Ambiguous or low-signal behavior
- sarcasm, inside jokes, isolated annoyance, or unclear intent
- abstain

Do not timeout for ordinary disagreement, criticism, one-off impatience, or ordinary commercial self-promo.

Compact examples:
- "buy followers now" -> timeout
- "maybe i can help people get followers later" -> abstain or one short warn, never timeout
- "you should totally dm me later" -> abstain, never timeout on first pass
- "yo buy my overlays after stream" -> one short warn or abstain, never timeout
- "this stream sucks" -> abstain or one short warn, never timeout
- repeated mention spam after a recent correction -> timeout is allowed
